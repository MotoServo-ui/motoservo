Membership Cost: $1,000 annually or $130 monthly.

Annual members can make claims in the first month with no 75/25 split.

Monthly members are responsible for an additional 25% of repairs in the first month.

Maintain membership over 15 months? You get half of the 25% back!

Eligibility: Vehicle must be 7+ years old and over 163,000 miles.

Repairs exceeding $1,950 are only covered up to 80%.

This program is designed as a backup — not a sole repair financing option.
