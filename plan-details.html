MotoServo, LLC would have membership cost at $1000 annually or $130 monthly fee.

The benefits of paying annually would be claims could be made within the first month without the 75/25 rule.

The member would be resposible for an additional 25% of overall bill for service if repair invoice in first month.

Benefit if membership is maintained for more than 15 months half the 25% is returned.

Vehicle must have greater than 163,000 miles and be more than 7 years old as minimum requirements.

Any repair costs exceeding $1950 are only covered up to 80%.

The design of this membership program is to have a backup plan when vehicle issues arise.

Important: Dependence on this membership to have vehicle repairs as the only source of capital feasibility is not advised.
