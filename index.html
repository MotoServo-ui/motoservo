About MotoServo

MotoServo is a startup dedicated to advancing silicon wafer technology through research, development, and precision engineering. Founded with the belief that innovation in semiconductor materials drives global progress, we focus on creating scalable, sustainable, and cutting-edge wafer solutions.

Our mission is to combine scientific curiosity with engineering excellence — building the foundation for tomorrow’s electronics, clean energy systems, and advanced computing applications.
