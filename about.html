About Us

MotoServo was born out of a deeply personal frustration with the automotive industry. After owning a vehicle for 13 years, I was faced with $2,200 in repair costs. When I turned to a dealership for a solution, I was offered only $400 — far below the vehicle's Kelley Blue Book value. It felt like an unfair tradeoff: either take a lowball offer or go through the hassle of listing the car, negotiating with strangers, and waiting for a buyer.

In that moment, I thought — there has to be a better way.

What if there were a plan in place that could help cover major repair costs before they happened? What if drivers could stay on the road with confidence, knowing they were protected?

That’s why I created MotoServo — to give car owners peace of mind and a smarter alternative to sudden, out-of-pocket repair expenses or unfair trade-ins. Our goal is to keep you driving longer, smarter, and without the pressure of unexpected repair bills.
