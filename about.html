What Started It All

It started when our founder walked away from a dealership offer that undervalued his trusty 13‑year‑old car. Instead of settling, he created MotoServo — a community where owners share costs and keep their vehicles running longer, without predatory warranties or trade‑in pressure.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 in Saint Joseph, Missouri, MotoServo was born out of real-world frustration and firsthand insight. While working as a parts manager in an animal food processing plant, the founder witnessed how critical systems, logistics, and downtime directly impacted business — and how costly it could be when technology failed. That same principle increasingly applies to vehicles today. With more repair shops opening to meet demand, many still lag behind when it comes to diagnosing and repairing modern vehicles driven by software and electronics.

MotoServo isn’t just a repair assistance service — it’s a strategic response to the evolution of the automobile itself. Gone are the days of the shade tree mechanic, where a socket wrench and know-how were enough. Today’s vehicles are rolling computers. ECUs, sensors, and networked modules now control everything from ignition timing to braking. As technology in cars advanced, the barrier to affordable repair also grew.

There’s no better symbol of American grit and ingenuity than the automobile industry. From replacing the horse-drawn carriage to becoming the backbone of national mobility, cars once gave every American a sense of independence. But what was once a luxury is now a necessity — and yet many can't afford the cost when something goes wrong.

That’s where MotoServo steps in: bridging the gap between modern vehicle complexity and affordable repair coverage. We're here to make sure drivers of older, high-mileage cars aren’t left behind in a world of high-tech transportation.
